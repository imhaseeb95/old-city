WHAT WE DO

HOW WE HELP RETAIL PARTNERS

We have been showing companies creative ways to monetize their properties during the pandemic with additional revenue streams.

We work with national brands that are looking to connect with popular retailers. They are looking to educate consumers about their products and services while providing your customers with free gift cards redeemable at your location. They set up a small table at your location (inside or outside – weather permitting) to interact with interested customers.

We employ the “Crawl – Walk – Run” strategy. Once you fully see the potential, we can expand the partnership. You have 100% control of what companies can participate and how often they can visit. The more days, the more money for you and the more gift cards we can hand out to your customers. Our average host location hosts 2-3 times per week.
